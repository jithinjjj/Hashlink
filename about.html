About Us

Hashlink aims to address the increasing need for a comprehensive and efficient tool to search and retrieve information from various social media platforms.The purpose of the Social Media Search Engine is to simplify the process of finding relevant information by providing users with a centralized platform to conduct searches across multiple social media channels.The objective of the project is to provide users with a convenient and efficient means of accessing information on social media. Whether users are looking for specific keywords, trending hashtags, or accounts associated with a particular phone number or email ID, the search engine will deliver accurate and up-to-date results, saving users time and effort in their search endeavour.
